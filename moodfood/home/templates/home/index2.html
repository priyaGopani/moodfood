About

We realized that finding the right food based on how you feel might not always be the easiest thing to do. Unfortunately, our different moods make us in the mood for different food places. But what are they? We introduce to you Mood Food.

Our Team

We are team Mood Food. We are three RIT students in three different computing majors; Priya (Computing Security) , Niki (Computer Science), and Angela (Software Engineering). We went to BrickHacks, a hackathon at RIT and after a conversation we realized that our moods affect what we want to eat, but sometimes it is not so easy to figure out what food that might be. This website we hopefully the lives of people easier. It will definitely make our lives easier.

The Approach

First you select your mood. Then you select your food based on your mood. Then we give you options of where to eat and the information you need to get to that place. It is that easy!
